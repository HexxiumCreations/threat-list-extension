FAQ


Why am I seeing this?

The website you tried to access has been (black)listed as malicious. This can be because of 1. Malware 2. Scamming 3. Deceptive Content etc.


Why did MY website get blocked?

Malicious Domain/IP blocker fetches blacklisted domains from the HexxiumCreations Threat database. If you believe your website should not be on this list, go to https://hexxiumcreations.com/request-domain-ip-reclassification/.


How do I turn this extension off?

Click on the extension box next to your url bar, then go to settings, and press the (green) symbol. To turn the extension back on again, do the exact same.


How do I remove this extension?

You can easily remove this extension from chrome by right clicking the extension's symbol next to the url bar. Then click "Remove from chrome..."


How do I add a malicious domain to the blacklist?

The extension fetches blacklisted domains from the HexxiumCreations threats database, all malicious domains must be submitted there. Go to https://hexxiumcreations.com/submit-malicious-domains/ to submit a malicious domain.


What information is being stored and where?

We do not track or save web-traffic. Our extension reads the url the outgoing request is pointing to, and compares it to the threats database. The only information stored are your settings and some session cache. This information is hosted by google and synced through your google account. Be aware that none of this information is encrypted, nor is it accessible to us.
