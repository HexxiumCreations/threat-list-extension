FAQ


Why am I seeing this?

The website you tried to access has engaged in malicious activity. This can be because of Malware, scams, and other content deceptive or harmful to users.


What do I do if this is my website?

Malicious Domain Blocker fetches blacklisted domains from the Hexxium Creations Threat database. If you believe your website should not be on this list, please contact us at support@hexxiumcreations.com


How do I turn this extension off?

Click on the extension box next to your url bar, then go to settings, and press the (green) symbol. To turn the extension back on again, do the exact same.


How do I remove this extension?

You can easily remove this extension from chrome by right clicking the extension's symbol next to the url bar. Then click "Remove from chrome..."


How do I add a malicious domain to the blacklist?

You can submit a domain or IP you consider malicious here


What information is being stored and where?

We do not track or save web-traffic. Our extension reads the url the outgoing request is pointing to, and compares it to the threats database. The only information stored are your settings and some session cache. This information is hosted by google and synced through your google account. Be aware that none of this information is encrypted, nor is it accessible by us.
